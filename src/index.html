Our Services

Life Cycle Assessments (LCA)

We perform comprehensive cradle-to-grave LCAs to quantify a product's environmental impact across every stage — from raw material extraction to end-of-life.

Using ISO 14040/44 methodology, we help you:

  • Identify environmental hotspots in your value chain
  • Compare design and material alternatives
  • Support eco-design and circularity strategies
  • Prepare for regulatory and customer sustainability requirements

Our LCAs transform complex data into actionable insights - enabling upstream supply chain mapping, identification of embodied carbon reduction pathways, optimization of after sales revenue streams and the design of efficient end-of-life product strategies.

Environmental Product Declarations (EPD)

We prepare verified Environmental Product Declarations that communicate your product's footprint transparently and credibly.

EPDs are increasingly demanded in construction, manufacturing, and public procurement, especially under EU green public procurement rules.

We support you throughout the process — from data collection and LCA modeling to third-party verification — ensuring your EPD meets all relevant standards (EN 15804, ISO 14025).

Our Comprehensive Services:

Quantifying GHG Emissions (Scopes 1, 2, & 3)

We meticulously quantify your greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions across all three scopes, adhering to the internationally recognized GHG Protocol and ISO 14064 standards.

  • Scope 1: Direct emissions from sources owned or controlled by your company
  • Scope 2: Indirect emissions from the generation of purchased energy
  • Scope 3: All other indirect emissions in your value chain
Key Deliverables:
  • Developing Corporate & Product-Level Carbon Inventories
  • Identifying Emission Hotspots across operations and supply chains
  • Designing Net-Zero Strategies & Credible Decarbonization Roadmaps

Understanding Sustainability and the Future

The world is moving toward a new economic reality, one where environmental performance defines business success.

Governments and markets are demanding transparency on emissions, material use, and lifecycle impacts through frameworks like CSRD, CBAM, and ESPR.

Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) stands at the heart of this transformation. It's the science that helps us understand how every product, process, or service affects the environment — from cradle to grave.

By applying rigorous LCA practices, companies can:

  • Quantify the environmental impact of their products and services
  • Analyze and map supply chains to pinpoint carbon and waste hotspots across the value chain
  • Improve efficiency and resource use
  • Ensure credible sustainability communication through EPD verification and compliance with product-level disclosure requirements

As sustainability becomes a strategic and regulatory necessity, our mission is to equip businesses with the insights, data, and confidence they need to move forward responsibly — and competitively.
